Why 52-Week Range Position Matters More Than Price
When corn sits at 86% of its 52-week range versus natural gas at just 2%, it reveals relative value stories. High range positions often signal either strong fundamentals or overextended moves ready for correction. Low range positions can indicate either genuine weakness or opportunity. Smart producers track these percentages alongside absolute prices to gauge whether current levels offer value or risk.
