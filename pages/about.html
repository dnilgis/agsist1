About AGSIST

Agricultural Support Services built for the people who grow America's food.

What Is AGSIST?

AGSIST is a free agricultural dashboard built by Farmers First Agri Service, a crop insurance agency serving farmers across Wisconsin and Minnesota. The goal is simple: put the data farmers actually need in one fast, clean, ad-free place.

The Farmers First Ag Index

The FFAI is a proprietary weekly composite indicator that scores the current agricultural economic environment across six categories: commodity prices, input costs, weather risk, export demand, credit conditions, and policy outlook. It's published every Monday and available for free to any farmer who wants it.

Why Free?

Ag data has historically been locked behind expensive subscriptions designed for commodity traders, not the farmers who actually raise the crops. AGSIST exists to level that playing field. Every tool on this site is and will remain free.
