Cookie Policy

Last updated: February 2026

What We Use

AGSIST uses minimal browser storage. We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking.

localStorage (Not Cookies)

We store one item in your browser's localStorage:

  • agsist-theme — stores your dark/light mode preference ("dark" or "light"). This never leaves your browser and contains no personal information.

Google Analytics Cookies

Google Analytics 4 sets cookies to distinguish users and sessions for anonymous usage reporting. These cookies are:

  • _ga — expires 2 years, distinguishes users
  • _ga_XXXXXXXX — expires 2 years, session persistence

No personally identifiable information is included. You can opt out via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

No Advertising Cookies

AGSIST does not use advertising networks, retargeting pixels, or social media tracking cookies.

Managing Cookies

You can clear all cookies and localStorage through your browser settings at any time. This will reset your theme preference to dark mode.
