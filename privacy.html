Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Plain English We collect almost nothing. Your location stays in your browser. If you give us your email, we use it to send you what you asked for. We don't sell anything to anyone. That's it.

What We Collect

  • Location — your browser's geolocation or a ZIP code you type in. Used to show local weather and cash bids. Never leaves your browser, never hits our servers.
  • Email — only if you subscribe to the AGSIST Daily or FFAI newsletter. Used to send you exactly what you signed up for.
  • Analytics — anonymous page views via Google Analytics 4. No names, no personal info.

What We Do With It

Deliver weather, local bids, and market data relevant to where you farm. Send the daily briefing if you asked for it. That's the complete list.

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with anyone for any reason.

Third-Party Services

AGSIST uses these services to function. None of them receive your personal information:

  • Google Analytics 4 — anonymous usage stats
  • Open-Meteo — weather data
  • Formspree — processes signup forms
  • CoinGecko — cryptocurrency prices

Cookies

AGSIST stores one preference — your dark/light theme choice — using localStorage. It's not a tracking cookie and contains zero personal data.

Data Retention

Location data: never stored. Email: kept until you unsubscribe. Analytics: retained per Google's defaults (14 months).

Your Rights

Want off the email list? Reply "unsubscribe" to any AGSIST email, or contact me directly. Want anything else deleted? Same deal — just ask.
