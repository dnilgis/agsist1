The Farmers First Ag Index

The FFAI is a proprietary weekly composite that scores the current agricultural economic environment across six categories: commodity prices, input costs, weather risk, export demand, credit conditions, and policy outlook. Published every Monday, available to anyone who wants it.

Why Free
