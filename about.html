About AGSIST

Agricultural market intelligence built for farmers, not traders. One person, one mission — give grain producers the data tools they deserve.

What This Is

AGSIST is an agricultural intelligence dashboard. Live futures prices, local farm weather, spray conditions, urea volatilization risk, USDA reports, prediction markets, calculators, and a daily briefing — all in one place, no login required.

Good ag data has been locked behind expensive subscriptions designed for commodity desks, not for the farmers who actually raise the crops. I built this because I thought that was wrong, and because my own clients needed something better.
