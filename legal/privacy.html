Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

AGSIST collects only what is necessary to provide agricultural data and advisory services:

  • Location data — browser geolocation or ZIP code you provide, used solely to display local weather and cash bids. Never stored on our servers.
  • Email address — if you subscribe to the Farmers First Ag Index (FFAI) newsletter via our signup form.
  • Usage analytics — page views and interactions via Google Analytics 4. No personally identifiable information is collected.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to deliver weather forecasts, local cash bids, and market data relevant to your location. Email addresses are used only to send the weekly FFAI update you requested. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

AGSIST uses the following third-party services to function:

  • Google Analytics 4 — anonymous usage statistics. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Open-Meteo — weather forecast data. No personal data is transmitted.
  • Stooq.com — delayed commodity price data. No personal data is transmitted.
  • Formspree — email form processing. See Formspree's Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies

AGSIST uses a single localStorage key (agsist-theme) to remember your dark/light theme preference. This is not a tracking cookie and contains no personal information. See our Cookie Policy for details.

5. Data Retention

Location data is never stored beyond your browser session. Email addresses are retained until you unsubscribe. Analytics data is retained per Google Analytics defaults (14 months).

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your email from our newsletter list at any time by replying to any FFAI email with "unsubscribe." For other requests, contact us at privacy@agsist.com.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at privacy@agsist.com or through our contact page.
